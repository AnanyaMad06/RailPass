How RailPass Works


1. Student Registration

Students create accounts and submit required documents for verification.

2. College Verification

Colleges validate and approve applications instantly through a secure admin panel.

3. Digital Pass Issuance

Approved users receive a digital concession letter with a QR code.

4. Railway Verification

Present the digital pass at railway counters for quick and verified approval.
